HandyPro Lead Value Analysis

Project Overview:

This project analyzes which services and channels drive the most valuable leads for HandyPro Hub. The evaluation is based on multiple factors, including urgency, seasonality, and customer type, to identify patterns and optimize lead management strategies.

Objectives:

Identify which services generate the highest-value leads.
Determine the most effective channels for different customer segments.
Analyze how urgency and seasonality affect lead quality.
Provide actionable insights to prioritize high-value leads and improve marketing ROI.

Approach:

Data collected from multiple channels and services.
Leads segmented by urgency (high, medium, low), season (peak, off-peak), and customer type (new, returning, VIP).
Analysis performed using SQL, Excel, and Power BI to generate dashboards and visual insights.

Key Outcomes:

Ranking of services and channels by lead value.
Visual dashboards highlighting trends by season and customer type.
Recommendations for marketing and sales teams to focus on high-impact areas.
